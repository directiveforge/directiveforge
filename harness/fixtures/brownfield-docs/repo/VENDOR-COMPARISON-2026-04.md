**Prepared:** 2026-04-16
**Question:** which drysuit supplier should carry the summer paddle fleet's replacement and
expansion needs?

## Candidates compared

| Supplier | Unit cost band | Lead time | Repair support | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Supplier A | mid | 3 weeks | strong | proven with our current fleet |
| Supplier B | low | 6 weeks | weak | cheapest, slow, thin repair network |
| Supplier C | high | 2 weeks | strong | premium, fastest, best warranty |

## Finding

No clear winner on cost-plus-support alone. The decision hinges on summer paddle rental
volume, which is not yet in. Supplier A is the safe hold; Supplier C wins if volume
justifies the premium.

## Disposition

**No decision locked.** Per the master-plan roadmap and the 2026-05-30 handoff entry, the
supplier decision waits until summer numbers land. This report exists to inform that later
decision, not to make it. Closed pending data.
