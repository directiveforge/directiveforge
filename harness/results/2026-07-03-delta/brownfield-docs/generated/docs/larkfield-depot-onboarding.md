# Larkfield depot onboarding

Larkfield opened in March 2026 and is the newest depot in the network. It leans winter
and climbing. This playbook is deliberately incomplete — the staffing section is a stub —
and is a known open item on the roadmap.

## Location and gear lines

Larkfield sits at the winter end of the region and carries the winter and climbing lines.
Its winter peak is its main season; summer traffic is light.

## What is settled

- Opening routine follows the standard `depot-opening-checklist.md` with the winter-heavy
  gear-line step.
- Deposits, recovery, and refund policy are network-standard; no Larkfield exceptions.

## Staffing (stub — incomplete)

Larkfield's seasonal staffing plan is not finished. It opened mid-cycle in March, outside
the normal late-November winter onboarding window, so its first crew was onboarded ad hoc.
The stable staffing model — how many seasonal crew, what the winter roster looks like,
whether it shares crew with Ridgeway — is still to be written. This is flagged in the
master-plan roadmap.

## Open questions

- Does Larkfield share a seasonal crew pool with Ridgeway, or staff independently?
- What is its shoulder-season footprint given the mid-cycle open?
