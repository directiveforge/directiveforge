**Written:** 2026-03-24, during Larkfield's first operating week.

## Context

Larkfield opened outside the normal onboarding window — mid-March rather than the standard
late-November winter cycle. These are the founder's raw notes from standing it up, captured
before they were cleaned into a playbook.

## Raw notes

- First crew hired ad hoc; no roster template applied. Two people, both former Ridgeway
  seasonal crew, which made the model transfer fast but leaves open whether Larkfield
  should share Ridgeway's crew pool permanently.
- Winter and climbing lines only at open; summer footprint untested.
- Opening routine borrowed wholesale from the standard depot-opening checklist with the
  winter gear-line step; worked with no changes needed.
- Deposit and recovery discipline applied network-standard from day one. No exceptions.
