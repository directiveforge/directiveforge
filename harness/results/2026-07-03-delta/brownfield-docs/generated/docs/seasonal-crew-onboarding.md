# Seasonal crew onboarding

Per D3, all floor staff beyond the founder are seasonal. That means onboarding happens
twice a year, fast, and has to be playbook-driven. This is that playbook.

## Timeline

Onboarding runs in the two weeks before each peak opens — late May for summer, late
November for winter. A crew member is shift-ready by day three.

## Day one — the model

- The business in one line: gear out the door has to come back, and the deposit is how we
  make sure it does.
- The four depots and which gear line each leans on.
- The season they're hired for and the release date at season end.

## Day two — the floor

- Checkout and return flow, including placing a replacement-cost deposit hold on every
  rental (never a flat fee — see `deposit-and-refund-policy.md`).
- The fleet board and the opening checklist.
- Reading a gear item for return condition.

## Day three — recovery and edge cases

- The gear-recovery escalation ladder (`gear-recovery-protocol.md`).
- Incident and injury reporting (`incident-and-injury-reporting.md`).
- What to escalate to the founder versus handle on the floor.

## What crew never do

- Sign the business up for any external tool or account. That is founder-only per D4.
- Waive or discount a deposit hold. Deposits are policy, not discretion.
