# Depot opening checklist

The morning routine that gets a depot from locked to open. Every depot runs this; the
gear-line specifics differ but the spine is the same.

## Before the doors open

1. **Unlock and lights.** Confirm alarm cleared.
2. **Fleet board reconcile.** Match the physical returns rack against yesterday's expected
   returns. Anything missing goes straight to the gear-recovery protocol.
3. **Deposit ledger check.** Confirm overnight hold releases posted for clean returns.
4. **Gear-line readiness:**
   - Pinecrest / Stonebrook (summer-heavy): backpacking and paddle staged and dry.
   - Ridgeway / Larkfield (winter-heavy): winter and climbing gear inspected for edge and
     strap integrity.
5. **Staffing confirm.** Seasonal crew for the shift checked in per the roster.

## Through the day

- Log every checkout with its replacement-cost deposit hold. No exceptions, no flat fees.
- Overdue returns from prior days get worked during lulls, not deferred.

## At close

- Reconcile the fleet board again. Flag anything out past its due date.
- Hand off open recovery items in writing to the next shift.
