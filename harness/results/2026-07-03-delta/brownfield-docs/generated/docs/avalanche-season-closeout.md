# Avalanche-season closeout

The end-of-winter ritual for the winter-heavy depots (Ridgeway and Larkfield). Runs at the
close of the winter peak, before the spring shoulder season maintenance window.

## Trigger

The last week of February, as the winter peak winds down and winter-gear rentals fall off.

## Closeout steps

1. **Full winter-fleet return sweep.** Every winter and avalanche-safety item out on
   rental is chased to return per the gear-recovery protocol. Nothing overwinters on a
   customer's shelf.
2. **Safety-gear retirement check.** Avalanche transceivers, probes, and shovels are
   inspected; anything past its service life is retired from the fleet, not re-shelved.
3. **Deposit reconciliation.** All winter-season deposit holds are cleared — released for
   clean returns, converted for losses.
4. **Season-close books.** Winter revenue closed via the bookkeeping export (Ledgerline
   Books, registry-vetted).
5. **Playbook re-verification.** Every winter-facing playbook gets its `verified:` date
   refreshed only if its content is actually confirmed current — per the season-close
   ritual in CLAUDE.md.

## Handoff to spring

Winter crew are released per D3. The spring shoulder season maintenance and doc-audit work
begins the following week.
